About Us

Welcome to Alnoor Restaurant, your destination for Pakistani and Indian cuisine! We are a team of experienced professionals dedicated to bringing you the very best in authentic South Asian flavors. Our menu is carefully crafted to showcase the rich and diverse culinary traditions of both Pakistan and India, with a focus on using only the highest quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods. From savory biryanis and tender kebabs to rich curries and spicy chaats, our dishes are sure to transport your taste buds to the streets of Karachi, Lahore, or Mumbai.
